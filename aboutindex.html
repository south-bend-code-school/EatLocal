Why we created this website
South Bend has an abundance of amazing,unique local restaurants that are sometimes out-shined by big chains. Our site will not only bring attention to these places, but it will also highlight why they are special. We also know how difficult it can be to find a local restaurant. Our hope is that this website will provide easy access to the restaurants that support the community.
