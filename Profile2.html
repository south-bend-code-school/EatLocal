History

Purple Porch Co-op started with four neighbors who wanted access to local and organic food. They talked about the idea on the porch of one of the houses, this porch happened to be purple. Their talk turned into a small group of people who wanted to be a part of it. On Wednesdays and Saturdays, farmers and shoppers met at the building that holds Good Shepherd Montessori School. Purple Porch quickly outgrew that space and moved to the Lang Lab building, where it stayed for a few more years. Now the Purple Porch Co-op is at 123 Hill St, and includes a cafe and grocery. Purple Porch still has a market on Wednesdays and only sells local or organic options.
